ABOUT US

At LATTE-M, we're passionate about great coffee and great community. Since 2007, we've been crafting exceptional coffee experiences using ethically sourced beans and artisanal roasting techniques.

Our Coffee Journey

At LATTE-M, our journey began with a passion for exceptional coffee and a vision to create a cozy haven for coffee lovers. We source the finest beans from around the world, expertly roast them to perfection, and craft delightful coffee experiences.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We partner with ethical farms that prioritize environmental conservation and fair wages for workers.

Craftsmanship

Our master roasters carefully craft each batch to highlight the unique characteristics of every bean.

Community

We're dedicated to creating welcoming spaces where people can connect over exceptional coffee.
